# Compost system

## Physical setup
- **The pen** — 4x6 structure with raised walls, built by JET, dual chamber (left = active, right = finished)
- **The tumbler** — hexagonal rotating compost bin from Jim's Worm Farm, 2 internal sections, used for intake and "brewing"

## Intended workflow (as JET described it)
1. New material goes into the tumbler for intake and brewing
2. Over time, material moves from the tumbler into the pen's first (left) chamber
3. Once broken down, material moves to the pen's second (right) chamber as finished compost
